QUESTIONS WE HEAR FROM ENTERPRISE TEAMS
This is just consumer Starlink, right?
Absolutely not. Enterprise Starlink is engineered specifically for business-class infrastructure and heavy workloads. Unlike the consumer version, 2pifi's Enterprise Starlink includes public static IPv4 addresses, no sold-out area restrictions, and seamless integration with your existing routers, switches, and SD-WAN architecture.
Does 2pifi have a portal I can use?
Yes. 2pifi provides a comprehensive management portal, and unlike other providers, it is fully included in your standard monthly service charge. Through the portal, you can easily view data usage, performance metrics, antenna locations, uptime, and the current status of your equipment at any time.
Will this actually work at our locations?
We assess before we deploy. Satellite visibility, terrain, weather patterns, obstructions — all evaluated in our technical assessment. If a site isn't viable, we tell you before you commit. No wasted time, no wasted money.
FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
How long does deployment actually take?
▼
From the initial assessment to operational verification, a typical deployment takes 2 to 3 weeks. The site assessment process takes 3 to 5 business days, with installation and activation completing the rest of that window depending on your site count and complexity. If you have critical locations that need immediate connectivity, expedited options are available.
What happens if something goes wrong?
▼
You don't pay until it's fixed. We include 24/7 priority support as part of our service. If we miss our targets, we credit your account. If a site isn't viable during assessment, we tell you upfront with alternative recommendations.
Can you deploy across multiple sites simultaneously?
▼
Yes. We manage multi-site deployments as a coordinated project with dedicated teams. We've deployed to 30+ sites in parallel. The more sites, the better we can optimize logistics and reduce per-site deployment time. We'll discuss optimal deployment sequencing during your assessment.
Can I transfer Starlink accounts I already have deployed today?
▼
Yes, 100%. The migration process is easy, and 2pifi never charges a transfer fee. Just cancel your current service plan and unlock the antenna — we'll take it from there.
What ongoing support do you provide?
▼
24/7 priority support, proactive network monitoring, automatic failover management, and direct access to your deployment engineer. We also handle all firmware updates and hardware replacements under warranty. Your team doesn't manage the network alone.
What if our site isn't viable for Starlink?
▼
We identify this during the technical assessment — before you're committed to anything. If Starlink won't work, we'll recommend alternatives: fixed wireless, 5G, or wireline solutions. We're not in the business of forcing bad deployments. Your success is our success.
