Logistics

Travels With the Crew

Voyager changes everything — except logistics. It loads, transports, and deploys just like a manned zero-turn. No special equipment. No new trailers. Fits on your existing rig.

Crews move between properties in the same rhythm they always did. The only difference: no one is riding the mower. Set Voyager down, walk the perimeter once, press start, and move to the next job. Your crew handles the rest of the property while Voyager mows.

Setup

One-Time Smart Mapping

No geo-fencing. No app uploads. No complexity. Simply ride Voyager along the property perimeter one time. The robot learns the boundary, records the map, and saves it permanently to memory.

From that moment forward, Voyager mows that property autonomously, every week, without human input. RTK navigation keeps it straight. Cameras and LiDAR keep it safe. One property onboarded in 20 minutes. Your crews spend less time mapping, more time selling and executing additional services.
